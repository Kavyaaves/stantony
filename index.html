About Us

Our Legacy

Founded in 1994, St. Antony's Nursery & Primary School has been a beacon of quality education in Tondiarpet, Chennai for over 32 years. What started as a vision to provide accessible, nurturing education has grown into one of the most trusted institutions in the community.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to nurturing every child's potential through a balanced blend of academics, values, and creativity. Our experienced teachers provide a warm, supportive environment where young learners thrive and develop a lifelong love for learning.

What We Offer

From Pre. KG to 5th Standard, we offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to build strong foundations. Our focus on holistic development ensures that every child grows in knowledge, character, and confidence.
